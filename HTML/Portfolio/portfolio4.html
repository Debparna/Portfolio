Introduction

Designare was an organization that that I founded in the second year of my undergraduate of UC Davis. This organization was started with the sole purpose of bringing designer and engineers together to solve problems together.

The Challenge

To redesign the workcell application that serves as a connection between the robots and the workers.



My Role

I worked on this project with a fellow intern and two software engineers on my automation team. I was in charge of the re-design of the application, prototyping and coding the interface using react.js.

Problem

At the time when I joined college at University of California, Davis there was a lack of organizations that combined the interdisciplinary fields of design, technology and entrepreneurship. There was also a lack of a startup community and spirit in Davis.

Goal

Through this organization I wanted to:

  • Bring together designers and those interested in design to collaborate with students in computer science majors to work on passion projects together.
  • To educate students on the idea of creating side projects that could potentially turn into bigger projects or companies.
  • To get industry professionals in the field of product design, computer science and venture of come speak to students.
  • Separating the backend from the frontend and making a clear distinction between human and robot elements.

My Role

As the founder, I was involved in the recruiting, branding and marketing of the organization. I also created an online presence of the organization on social media and through coding and creating the website of Designare. We were a team of five designers and developers that created the presence of the organization on campus.
