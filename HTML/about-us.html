About me

Aye there, I am Debparna. I am a product focused developer and designer with entrepreneurial aspirations. In the past I have interned at Ripcord as a Kleiner Perkins Engineering Fellow, Tesla, Delphi Auto and Hegemony tech.

I am a rising senior at the University of California, Davis studying computer science with a concentration in design. In college I founded an organization called Dèsignare and was involved with StartupHub, HackDavis, Davis women in computer science. I have been able to teach myself a lot of skills outside of school including web development, UX Design, product design principals, data visualizations and machine learning. I consider myself passionate about things in the intersection of design and data science.

Design Mindset: Despite being tech minded I am a people person and empathize with people on a very personal level. I am able to establish a connection with people and users of products I am creating. This empathy helps me in the design thinking process as I am able to understand my user’s problems, goals and motivations.Having lived in different countries in my life I believe that my design philosophy stems a lot from all that I have witnessed and the problems that I have experienced. From my time living in India, I have learned to value creating accessible and inclusive designs.

Data Mindset: Being a developer and a computer science major has taught me how to problem solve. Given a problem, I am able to break it down top-down and understand the logical and technical constraints to solve these problems.

My Goal: My goal is to revolutionize the way people interact with products on an everyday basis.

When I am not designing or coding, I am thinking of ideas for ventures, writing on medium or taking photos.
